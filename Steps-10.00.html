3.     Steps to add the ‘Total Tour Months’ custom  column on our table.






  • On the Home rib go to Transform and right click ‘Tour ‘column then click on  Split column by digit to non-digit.
  • Select Tour.2 then click on  Split column by non-digit to a digit.
  •  click on Tour 2.1  press ctrl then click on Tour.3 right click select remove.
  •  Rename Tour.1 to Tour Year and Tour 2.2 to Tour Month.
  •  Replace null values from the Tour Month column with 0.
  •   The change data type for the Tour year and Tour Month column to the whole number.
  •  Then click on add a column from the home tab then select custom column. 
  •  Copy and paste this formula (Tour year * 12 + Tour Month) 
  • on custom column formula window. write ‘Total Tour Month’ on the new column name field.
  •  Then right-click on Total Tour Month’ and change the datatype to the whole number.
  •  Click on the file and select close and apply. Follow the Gif below step by step.
