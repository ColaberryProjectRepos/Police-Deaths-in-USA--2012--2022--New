Problem Statement 1

The United States has witnessed a significant number of police officer deaths in the past decade, with a total of 2612 Police officers losing their lives between 2012 and 2022. Among the leading causes of death, natural causes or job-related illnesses resulted in the highest number of deaths 51%, followed by gun violence resulted in 21% of deaths and Traffic-related incidents resulted in 18% of deaths.  The problem of police officer deaths is a serious concern that affects the entire law enforcement community, their families, and the public at large.
