Transforming the data in power Query Editor 9
In this step, you will clean and transform the data

1.     Transform the ‘End of watch’ column.     
  • In the Home ribbon, go to Transform and right-click on the ‘End of watch’ column then change the Data Type to Date. 
  • Filter 'End of watch' to have from  2012 to 2022. Right-click ‘End of watch’ click on Date filter then click on is after and type ‘01/01/2012’ then ok. 
  •  Sort it by Ascending order. Right-click on the ‘End of watch’ column the Sort by Ascending
  •   Rename table ‘Police deaths in USA from 2012 to 2022’. 
  •   Click on the file and select Close and apply.
